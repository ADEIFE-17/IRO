Report an Issue

Help improve AAUA and the surrounding communities by reporting issues affecting students, residents and public facilities. Your report will be assigned a unique Complaint ID which can later be used to monitor its progress.

Before You Submit

Please provide accurate information to help the appropriate authority understand and resolve the issue more effectively.


Reporting Tips

  • • State the exact location.
  • • Describe the issue clearly.
  • • Upload evidence if available.
  • • Avoid offensive language.

Privacy Notice

Your information will only be used for processing complaints. You may also choose to submit your report anonymously.


What Happens Next?

After submission, a Complaint ID will be generated. Keep this ID safe because it will be required when tracking your report.
